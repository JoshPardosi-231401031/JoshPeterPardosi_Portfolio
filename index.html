The Story Behind the Data

I'm an Undergraduate Computer Science student at Universitas Sumatera Utara (GPA: 3.68/4.00), currently selected as a Data Scientist Cohort at Coding Camp powered by DBS Foundation — a structured, intensive program where I build hands-on expertise in data science from the ground up.
